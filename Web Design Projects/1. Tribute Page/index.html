Dr Abdul Rahman Al-Sumait

The picture of the man himself

Timeline of Dr Al Sumait's life:

  • 1947:Dr. Abd Al-Rahman bin Hamood Al-Sumait was born.
  • 1974:With a medical degree, he graduated with a diploma in Tropical Diseases from the University of Liverpool.
  • 1978:He practiced medicine at Montreal Public Hospital
  • 1981:He founded the Africa Muslim Agency, later renamed as "Direct Aid" society. The society provides extensive humanitarian assistance to impoverished Muslims throughout Africa. It has built 124 hospitals and dispensaries, 840 schools, 204 Islamic Centers, 214 women training centers and 2,200 mosques. The society has also established two colleges in Kenya and Zanzibar, offered 200 scholarships to Muslim African students to pursue higher studies in medicine, engineering and technology. Direct Aid has its offices in 29 African countries. In addition to that, it was considered a General Consultant in the Economical and Social Board of the United Nations in 1998.
  • 2013:Dr Al sumait sadly passed away due to a heart condition he suffered with.
